Every feature was designed by a fleet operator, for fleet operators. No bloat, no accounting degree required.

Pay Period Tracker
Set your weekly income and track every dollar against planned vs. actual spending. Know your balance in real time.
📄
Bills & Loan Manager
Add all your monthly bills and equipment loans. Get alerts when something is due tomorrow so you never miss a payment.
💵
Transaction Log
Log fuel, maintenance, tolls, and any other spend as you go. Filter by category, search by vendor, and see where money is going.
🚛
Fleet Maintenance Log
Track service records per unit — oil changes, tire replacements, repairs, inspections. Full history with cost and mileage.
🔧
Maintenance Budget
Set a weekly maintenance fund and track deposits vs. expenses week by week. Never let the fund run dry unexpectedly.
📊
Weekly CSV Export
Export everything — transactions, bills, fleet logs — as a CSV file every week. Open it in Excel or Google Sheets to archive.

How it works
Up and running
in 2 minutes.
1
Open the App
Go to the URL on any phone or computer. No download, no app store, no account needed.
2
Enter Your Info
Add your business name, number of trucks, and weekly income on the setup screen. Takes 60 seconds.
3

Run Your Business
Log transactions as you go. Check your balance. Track maintenance. Export weekly. That's it.
